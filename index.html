Our Story

Fitness is more than
physical — it is personal.

At Faith & Form, we believe movement is a practice of self-respect. The discipline of showing up — for Stretch, Pilates, or Strength — builds not just your body, but your confidence, your clarity, and your character.

What started as a small idea has grown into a supportive community where anyone, no matter their fitness level, can feel encouraged to grow. Every step forward is worth celebrating.

✦

Faith

Believe in yourself and your ability to grow. Every journey starts with a single step and the courage to take it.

◈

Strength

Build both physical and mental resilience. True strength comes from consistency, not perfection.

◎

Community

Lift each other up and celebrate every win. We grow stronger together than we ever could alone.

The Space

Studio Policy
◷

Arrival

Please arrive at least FIVE minutes before class; once class begins, the doors will be locked.

◈

Cancellation

Classes must be cancelled at least five hours in advance of the class. If you cancel within the policy guidelines, your class will be credited back to your account.

✦

Late Cancel

If you need to "late cancel" (less than 5 hours before class), we get it. Things come up! However, due to our policy guidelines, we will not be able to credit this class back to your account.

⊘

No Show

If you "NO SHOW" a class (you do not late cancel or notify the instructor), you will lose that class and be subject to a $10 "NO SHOW FEE."

Please reach out with any questions! We are happy you are here.
